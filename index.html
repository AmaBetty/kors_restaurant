Our Philosophy

A Passion for Flavour, Rooted in Craft

Founded in 2008 by Chef Élise Morin, Maison Dorée was born from a dream to bring the warmth of a French countryside kitchen to city dining — without sacrificing artistry or ambition.

Every dish on our menu tells a story. We work directly with local farms, foragers, and fishermen to bring you ingredients at their absolute peak. Our kitchen is a laboratory, a stage, and a sanctuary all at once.

We believe that great food is not just about taste — it's about the memory it creates. From the first amuse-bouche to the final petit four, every detail is intentional.
